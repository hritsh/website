About this website:

This website is a responsive recreation of one of the original MySpace profiles from like 2005. It's based off of the original MySpace profile of Tom Anderson, the founder of MySpace.

Full disclosure I was a toddler or something when MySpace was a thing, so this isn't out of nostalgia, but I've always been fascinated by the internet of the 2000s and the culture surrounding it, so I decided to make this website as a fun little project. It's kind of nostalgic even though it's not? If that makes any sense.

I first started making it in Next.js and Tailwind, and was deploying it as a static site on Vercel, but I felt like I was over-engineering it. (Also because JavaScript bad) So I decided to rewrite it in vanilla HTML and CSS to make it more authentic and to kinda get a better understanding of how websites were made back then.
