About

Hello! I'm Hritish, I love tech & building stuff that leverages technology to solve real-world problems, especially at the intersection of software engineering, machine learning & healthcare.

I'm currently working at Circum Medtech as a Machine Learning Engineer, where I'm working on a smart catheter system that uses ML to detect sepsis early in ICU patients.

My work involves training models on large healthcare datasets, developing real-time data pipelines with ICU systems, and building a desktop & mobile app for clinicians to monitor patient vitals.

When I'm not working or building personal projects, I enjoy exploring new technologies, ricing my desktop, and spending hours writing scripts to automate stuff that take 5 minutes to do.
